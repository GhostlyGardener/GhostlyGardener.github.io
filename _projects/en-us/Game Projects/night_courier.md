<b>Overview</b><br/>
This is a 2D top-down stealth game set in Liaodong in 1619, on the eve of the Battle of Sarhū. As a Ming dynasty night scout, the player must cross slip through enemy territory to uncover troop movements and return with information that may change the fate of the border garrison. The experience focuses on stealth, pressure, isolation, and the feeling of carrying a mission larger than yourself.<br/><br/>
