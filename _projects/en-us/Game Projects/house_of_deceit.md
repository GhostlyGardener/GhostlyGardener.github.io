<b>Overview</b><br/>
You wake up in the dark and find yourself bound hand and foot, trapped in a room with a suspicious figure wearing a strange mask.
Something has been stuffed into your mouth, leaving you unable to speak. You can only answer the figure’s questions by nodding or shaking your head.<br/>

“Do you trust me?”<br/>
“Do you like me?”<br/>
“Do you want to leave?”<br/>

You may answer directly, or investigate the truth using special tools.<br/>The truth, along with each “yes” or “no” choice, seems to determine your future.<br/>

---

<b>Gameplay</b><br/>
This game is played mostly with the mouse.<br/>

- Investigation: Click the investigation option, then drag on the screen to create a window.<br/>Confirm the selected area and inspect highlighted objects to collect clues.<br/>
- Dialogue: Click dialogue box to continue conversations.<br/>
- Clues: Click clues to review the information you have collected.<br/>
- Esc: Open the settings menu.<br/>

<b>Notes</b><br/>
Created during Global Game Jam<br/>

- Ending 1: Choose to stay at the end.
- Ending 2: Choose to leave at the end.
- True Ending: Discover all key clues through precise investigation.
- Other Ending: Do not investigate, then choose Trust → Like → Stay.
