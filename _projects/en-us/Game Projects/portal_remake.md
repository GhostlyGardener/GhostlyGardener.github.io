<b>Overview</b><br/>
The game features five levels that gradually build on the core portal mechanics. As players progress, they become familiar with portal placement, traversal, and momentum changes, learning to use these systems to cross obstacles, redirect movement, and reach spaces that would otherwise be inaccessible. As the game moves forward, new mechanics, tools, and enemies are introduced, expanding the range of situations in which the portal system can be used.<br/><br/>

---

<b>Controls</b><br/>
WASD to move<br/>
Space to jump<br/>
Right / Left click to shoot portals<br/>
R to reset portals<br/>
F to skip dialogue<br/>
ESC to free mouse<br/>
