<b>Overview</b><br/>
This underground world once had color. Light passed through stained-glass structures, casting vibrant hues across its caves, ruins, and cities; now, only gray-white remains and silence are left behind. As the light gradually faded, civilization collapsed, and its inhabitants regressed into faintly glowing beings in the dark.<br/><br/>

The player awakens among the ruins, carrying two devices while moving through the fallen world: a miniature stained-glass tool that can paint the environment in red, blue, and green, and a Lightbug Bottle that releases faint light energy. Using these two abilities, the player solves environmental puzzles, opens the way forward, and gradually comes to understand the world’s history and decline.<br/><br/>
